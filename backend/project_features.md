# Khela Dekho Blog - Project Overview

Khela Dekho (which translates to "Watch the Game") is an online sports blogging platform. It allows sports fans, writers, and journalists to write, read, and manage articles about various sports.

Below is an overview of the features built for this platform:

---

## 👥 User Accounts & Access Control

*   **Sign-Up & Log-In:** Users can register for a new account and securely log in. The system keeps users logged in safely using secure session keys.
*   **Writers and Administrators:** The platform supports different types of users:
    *   **Writers (Standard Users):** Can publish articles, edit their own articles, and delete their own articles.
    *   **Administrators:** Have administrative privileges, such as creating new sports categories and managing other user accounts.

---

## 🗂️ Sports Categories

To keep the blog organized, every article is associated with a specific sport category.
*   **Pre-defined Sports:** The platform comes pre-seeded with 22 different sports categories, including Cricket, Football, Badminton, Tennis, Swimming, Chess, Wrestling, and E-Games.
*   **Admin Management:** Administrators can add new sports categories as the platform grows.

---

## ✍️ Article Management

Writers can manage their posts with the following features:
*   **Creating Articles:** Writers can publish articles by specifying a title, main content, and choosing a sports category.
*   **Editing & Deleting:** Writers can update the text or delete their articles at any time.
*   **Media & Reference Links:** Articles support attaching images, video links, or external references to make the content richer.

---

## 🔍 Smart Search

Readers can easily find articles of interest:
*   **Keyword Searching:** Readers can type in keywords or phrases to search through all articles.
*   **Relevance Sorting:** The search feature automatically ranks and displays the most relevant articles first based on the search terms.

---

## ☁️ Media Uploads

*   **Image Attachments:** Authors can attach images directly to their posts.
*   **Fast and Secure Uploads:** Images are uploaded directly to secure cloud storage. The system restricts uploads to standard image types (like JPEG and PNG) and limits file sizes to prevent spam.
